Project Overview

Transformer Audio Classifier

Brain Inspired ML Final Project

Team: Akhil, Chris, Eisha, Jason, & Lakshman

97% Accuracy achieved with our Transformer-based model

This Audio Classifier represents our second attempt at successfully classifying audio samples of country names. Our initial LSTM model had low accuracy, prompting us to explore Transformer architecture, which yielded significantly better results.

Audio Feature Extraction

MFCC Features

Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients with delta and delta-delta features for spectral analysis.

Mel-Spectrogram

Mel-scale spectrogram with 128 frequency bands for detailed frequency analysis.

Chroma Features

Chroma vectors for pitch class analysis and harmonic content detection.

Spectral Features

Centroid, rolloff, bandwidth, and zero-crossing rate for spectral characteristics.

Harmonic/Percussive

Separated harmonic and percussive components for detailed audio analysis.

Spectral Contrast

Spectral contrast and tonnetz features for timbral analysis.
